About Us

What's different?

Living the nomadic lifestyle isn’t easy. Life takes turns, and Nomad is there for you. We promise to be there when plans fall through. Our hotels average a radius of one per every 180 miles, that way if the sun is setting on another hard day, within only a few hours you’ll be finding one of our locations.

We are the Hotel for the Modern Nomad

We can make the promise that you’ll be able to stay on the premisis until we can help you settle down for a while. We allow, and even encourage, our employees to live the lifestyle that you do. Those who take advantage can work at almost any of our locations.

Our history

In 2012 George McCarthey, tired of living out of stinky motels and driving for days on end, founded Nomad. He looked at himself for what those living the lifestyle would have wanted. Nothing too fancy or extravagant. They wanted namely the bare essentials, however, something more luxurious could be desired after many days of travelling. The parking lot was enlarged at the smaller locations so that more nomads could park their vehicles. McCarthey even wanted to start giving to local non-profits that fit with the values of the business and help those in need.

Our Offers

Even if you live on the seat of your pants, you can come in and buy a night’s stay, or you could buy nights in bulk. Sometimes plans fall through, and we know that having those nights might allow you to stay out of that random parking lot for just another day or two. These nights are able to be redeemed at any of our locations, and they never expire, so you can stretch them out!

Have stories?

Every so often we may give you a card, we want you to write for our blog! Tell us why you chose this life. Tell us about your favorite trip. Tell us about the hardships. We want to give an honest and accurate account of being a nomad. We only ask that you keep it clean and friendly.
